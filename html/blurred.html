Real-world impact

Visual impairments affect more than just sight. They influence confidence, learning speed, error rates, and even social participation. In digital systems, poor accessibility can create unnecessary barriers for millions of users.

Design takeaway

Good accessibility design reduces reliance on perfect perception. Features like contrast control, scalable UI, and redundant feedback systems make interfaces usable for everyone—not just ideal users.

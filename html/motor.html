What this simulation represents

This simulation represents reduced motor precision and delayed input response. As the delay increased, aiming and clicking accurately became more difficult.

Real-world impact

Motor impairments can affect reaction speed, accuracy, hand movement, and the ability to use a mouse or controller comfortably. Games that rely on fast and precise input can become frustrating or inaccessible without support options.

Design takeaway

Features such as larger targets, aim assist, slower gameplay options, remappable controls, and alternative input methods can reduce barriers and make games more accessible.
